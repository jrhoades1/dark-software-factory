## Quick Decision Tree

```
Customer needs pool leak detection?
├── Residential?
│   ├── Pool only or spa only, 1 skimmer → $395
│   ├── Pool/spa with spillover, 1 skimmer → $495
│   └── Has extra features? → Add fees from feature table
│       └── 3+ features? → QUOTE FROM TECH
└── Commercial?
    ├── How many gallons? How many skimmers/gutters?
    │   └── Use commercial pool table
    └── Fountain? → GO SEE 1ST

Customer needs slab leak / potable water detection?
├── Residential?
│   └── How many bathrooms? → Use potable water table
└── Commercial?
    └── Use commercial hourly rate ($850 + $200/hr)

Customer needs source & origin?
├── Residential?
│   ├── 1 problem, ≤20 sq ft → $550
│   └── Multiple problems or 5+ bath → $650/$200
└── Commercial?
    └── Use commercial hourly rate ($850 + $200/hr)
```

> Prices are for the franchise of Palm Beach, Martin, & St. Lucie Counties only. Each office is independently owned and operated.
